BluePi LMS offers a unique proposition, where an organization or an individual can create a complete Learning Management System in a few short steps. All you need to do is to sign up!! Once you sign up, a complete LMS is created and deployed behind the scenes. Utilizing the Cloud Computing infrastructure, the LMS is ready to use in matter of a few minutes.

Key Features

  • Speed to Market : From the time you sign up it takes less than half an hour to get the LMS working. You can tailor the look and feel and change the branding of the site, which also can be done within minutes. This gives you unparalleled speed to market.

  • Concentrate on things that really matter : Since the development is taken care of automatically behind the scenes, you can concentrate on things such as quality of content, user enrollment etc. without having to worry about the technology, deployment, up-gradation of the system, support and maintenance etc.

  • Cost effective : Along with saving on the development cost, the system is subscription based, which further drives down the cost, since the subscription costs are very low as compared to hosting on a propriety or an in house system.

  • Modify the look and feel, including the branding : Administrator can log in and modify the look and feel of the system as well as the branding. There is more details included in the later section of this guide, on what can be changed and how to do that.
